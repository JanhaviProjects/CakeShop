About Us

Beautiful Story

As we all desire to surprise our beloved ones at midnight, we seek different portals that deliver midnight birthday cakes to our doorstep. Also, if they do same day cake delivery, then if not at midnight, we want to send our love at least on the main day. Beleniss is one such online cake bakery in India that allows you to choose from a comprehensive collection of cakes and send them to the doorstep at a preferred time. It may be on the same day or midnight, you just need to add the delivery address, choose the delivery time, and we will love to pamper your loved ones with our lip-smacking cakes. We don’t charge a penny for same day cake delivery. Yes, there are no hidden charges here. Apart from that our midnight cake delivery charges are Rs 249, and the time slot that we offer is from 11 PM-1 AM. So, you can begin your celebration sharp at 12 o’clock with Beleniss prompt delivery services. Order today and dive into the flavours.
